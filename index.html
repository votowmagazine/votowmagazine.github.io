The Launch
17.03.26

Welcome to VOTOW! We are happy to announce the first full issue: Issue 0: The Fool, will release in one month from today, the 17th of April, 2026! Each week going forward we will be releasing this issue's art prints and promotional art as a sample of what is to come. You can find them on our art page and on all of our social media platforms, along with a variety of other teasers, treats and more as we make our way to the release.

On top of that, the first issue's digital version will be released completely free, along with a single audio version of one of that issue's stories. Subscribers of the physical tiers will receive their first issue as well as the other bonuses in their particular tier! Back issues will be available for purchase as soon as the next issue is released on our releases page.

We look forward to releasing this into the world, it has been a long time coming.

— The VOTOW team.

About

The Voice of the Outer Worlds is a curated collection of speculative fiction, poetry and art delivered in a variety of audio and visual formats to our readers on a monthly basis. A collaboration between a variety of artists and writers, VOTOW brings you to the bleeding edge of speculative explorations of our world and beyond in a bespoke, high quality package.

Previous issues and individual art prints can be purchased one month after their release.

Want to have your art or writing featured in an issue of VOTOW? All works can be submitted via our contact page here.

Have general questions? Issues? You can reach out to us in any of the below ways:

@votowmag Voiceoftheouterworlds@proton.me
